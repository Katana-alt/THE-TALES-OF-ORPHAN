The Tales of an Orphan

By Kitsao Katana

“I didn’t choose to be an orphan. But I chose what kind of man I’d become.” In this heartfelt memoir, Kitsao Katana shares the story of growing up in Muyeye, a small village on Kenya’s coast, where loss came early and hope was hard-earned. Through the eyes of a boy learning to survive without parents—and later, a young man finding purpose in teaching—The Tales of an Orphan is a journey through grief, grit, and grace.
